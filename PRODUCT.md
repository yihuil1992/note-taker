# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Note Taker is for people who join frequent meetings on Windows and need a local-first way to capture audio, transcribe multilingual conversations, review summaries, and export records without sending data to the cloud by default.

## Product Purpose

The product is a working desktop console for meeting capture. Success means a user can start and stop recording, see whether local transcription assets are ready, process transcripts, review summaries, and export honest stored data with low cognitive overhead.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, archival, exact. The interface should feel like a local observatory console: dim, precise, and calm enough to leave open during a call. It should inherit Hoshikuzu's star-atlas atmosphere without becoming a sci-fi dashboard or decorative skin.

## Anti-references

Do not make the app look like a marketing dashboard, glassy AI product page, beige notes app, gradient-heavy SaaS mockup, or rounded card gallery. Avoid invented collaborators, avatars, analytics, storage metrics, or decorative AI-style filler unless the data exists.

## Design Principles

- Local trust stays visible: privacy, storage path, source readiness, and provider state should be legible without becoming banners.
- State earns color: pale cyan is reserved for selection, readiness, and focus; soft amber is reserved for the main recording path.
- The shell is an observatory: capture, archive, settings, and meeting detail appear as instruments in one star-atlas workspace, not as a generic four-column dashboard.
- Premium means restraint: use hairlines, solid surfaces, tight radii, and direct typography instead of gradients, wide shadows, or decorative layers.
- Empty and setup states stay honest: missing data should be useful and specific, never filled with fake product context.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve visible keyboard focus, clear disabled states, sufficient touch targets on mobile, reduced-motion alternatives, and color-independent meaning for important states.
